How to Fix Slow WiFi at Home in 10 Steps

Intro
Slow WiFi is frustrating, especially when you're working or streaming. Before you call your ISP, try these 10 fixes.

1. Restart Your Router and Modem

Unplug both devices for 60 seconds, then plug the modem back in first.

2. Check Your Internet Speed

Go to speedtest.net on your phone or laptop.
